The future of recycling is unstable

Inconsistent standards, practices and profitability are creating major challenges in recycling. If we alter the way we think about waste from a burden to a resource, we can create dramatic change. Recycling can work if we do, too.

Recycling Is A Success Story

Since the 1960s, increased rates of recycling has been one of the most widespread success stories in American conservation and sustainability.
